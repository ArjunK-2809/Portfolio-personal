Background

Electrical Engineer & VLSI Researcher

I'm an Electrical Engineering student at IIT Palakkad, currently working on Compute-In-Memory (CIM) research at IIT Madras under Dr. Janakiraman Viraraghavan, coordinated by Dr. Arvind Ajoy (IIT Palakkad).

Part of ISWDP Cohort 8 — the Industry Sponsored VLSI Design Programme. My work spans Analog CIM PCB design (KiCad, component selection, fab prep) and Digital CIM in TSMC 28nm (RTL-to-GDSII with Cadence Genus + Innovus, DRC/LVS/PEX, SRAM simulation).

I also work with Synopsys Sentaurus TCAD for physics-based semiconductor device simulations — from drift-diffusion to quantum corrections at scaled nodes.

Beyond circuits: self-improvement is my operating system. The gym, chess, football, cricket, basketball, and relentless curiosity keep me sharp.
